# Privacy Policy

**AI Clothes Changer**
**Last updated:** March 10, 2026

## Overview

AI Clothes Changer ("the App") respects your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

## Data We Collect

### Photos
- Photos you select are uploaded to our AI processing service (fal.ai) for image editing
- Photos are processed in real time and **not stored** after the result is delivered
- We do not use your photos for training, advertising, or any purpose other than generating your edit

### Anonymous User ID
- We create an anonymous account to manage your credits and purchases
- No personal information (name, email, phone) is collected or linked to this ID

### Device ID
- Used by RevenueCat to restore purchases across app reinstalls
- Not linked to your personal identity

### Purchase History
- Managed by RevenueCat to track credit balances and entitlements
- Linked to your anonymous account for functionality purposes only

### Diagnostics
- Crash logs and performance data may be collected automatically
- This data is anonymous and used solely to improve app stability

## How We Use Your Data

All data collected is used exclusively for:
- Providing the core AI image editing functionality
- Managing your credits and in-app purchases
- Improving app performance and fixing crashes

We do **not** use your data for:
- Advertising or marketing
- Tracking across apps or websites
- Selling to third parties

## Third-Party Services

- **fal.ai** — AI image processing (photos are not retained after processing)
- **RevenueCat** — In-app purchase management
- **Apple** — Payment processing (we never access your payment information)
- **Supabase** — Anonymous authentication and data storage

## Data Retention

- Photos: deleted immediately after processing
- Account data: retained while you use the app
- Diagnostics: aggregated and anonymized

## Your Rights

You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at josephzhang.works@gmail.com.

## Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.
