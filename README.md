## FAQ

### How does ImagineAI work?
Describe the outfit you want — or provide reference images — and Seedream 5.0 AI transforms your photo instantly.

### What is Seedream 5.0?
Seedream 5.0 is a next-generation AI image editing model with built-in reasoning. It understands your creative intent and delivers realistic results.

### How do I get more credits?
Open the app and tap the credits icon to purchase additional credits. Credits are consumable and shared across your account.

### Can I get a refund?
Refunds are handled by Apple. Visit [Apple Support](https://support.apple.com/en-us/HT204084) to request a refund.

### Is my data safe?
Your photos are processed securely and are not stored on our servers after generation is complete.

## Privacy Policy

ImagineAI does not collect personal data beyond what is required for account authentication and in-app purchases. Photos are uploaded for AI processing only and are not retained after the result is delivered.
